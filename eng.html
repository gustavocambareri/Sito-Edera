0. Manifesto Organico

           The organic manifesto, like a living creature, mutates and regenerates its own form, solidifying its being and at the same time reshaping its thinking, constantly questioning itself in order to shatter any Ideological veil.

1. Why Edera

           As ivy, which inspired our name, Edera, aims at permeating through the city’s wefts, creating a new environment in the reality of Milan and beyond, formed by people determined to build an elastic and stimulating context from an artistic, personal, cultural and social point of view.
           All this is only feasible through constant research and direct involvement of people with different kinds of abilities, sharing among all the concrete possibilities and space to fully express their inspiration.
           Edera’s goal is, therefore, to generate a human substrate that is as homogeneous in intentions as multifaceted in socio-cultural intersectionality.

2. Morphic Resonance

           Edera Collective believes in the existence of a shared memory, characterized by the stillness of the past, present, and future.
           Tapping philosophically into Rupert Sheldrake’s Morphic Resonance theory, Edera Collective believes that investigating the eternal bond between past and future dimensions is fundamental for establishing a direct connection with who will replace us in the future, looking out to what the past gave us and learning from it.

3. Natural Dualism

           Edera Collective embraces the dualistic conception of Nature conceived in its physical dimension as the foundation of existence and the living Principle operating and generating all things, and in its ideological facet, in which Nature propels Being in a process of Total Ethical Mimesis.

4. Unavoidability and Coexistence.

           In total affinity with the principles featured in the Manifesto of Rural Futurism, Edera deems it necessary to raise popular awareness regarding the conflicting areas of today’s dialectical relationship between society and the natural world.
           The key point of reflection is the internalization of these conflicting dimensions, to carry out a real process of eco-cultural revaluation of space, polarizing social consciousness toward a dimension of Coexistence.

5. Natural Suprematism

           By Natural Suprematism Edera means the supremacy of pure sensibility in the multimedia arts in relation to Nature. Natural phenomena acquire meaning within themselves at the moment they are involved in the universal and timeless process of Morphic Resonance.
           In the dialectic of Natural Suprematism the concretization of the sensibility of consciousness occurs through a representation that, in the creative path, loses its purely abstract and figurative dimension, thus rediscovering its objective essence.

6. Sound

           Sound allows us to contribute, through a cathartic and emotional process, to the internalization of the representative dimensions of our investigation, giving a voice to the seemingly silent world that is the environments in which we move.
            Moreover, Edera believes that Sound represents one of the real cultural and social aggregators, identifying it as a possible joining link between our inner self and the external world of the social community.

7. Light

           Light, the tangible substance that with its movement permeates everything and composes the Cosmos framework, is one of the central elements of Edera Collective.
           Light beams, capable of striking directly and instinctively, cleave darkness and allow the definitive liberation from the “Ricatto del Buio”: a condition in which every abandoned and unnecessary space it’s forced to perish in darkness and stasis.

8. Speculative Fantasy

           Edera embraces the use of Speculative Fantasy as a transcendent research engine, which is the necessary first step in the process of translating abstractions of Being into concrete form.
           The necessary condition to prevent this process from involuting into a sterile situationist rhetoric is the implication of critical and futuristic thinking.
            Speculation, therefore prediction; prediction, therefore Action.

9. Web Trip

           In an age of constant technological expansion, Edera envisions the Digital as an extension of Being and as a virtuous tool apt for the growth of social welfare. Edera detaches itself from a prejudicial rejection of technological progress, while instead probing the social potential for the expansion of Connective Intelligence inherent in the Asimov-ian Virtual Paraverse.

10. Free and accessible cultural fruition

           Edera believes that free and egalitarian access to culture is of vital importance, condemning all kinds of economic speculation, both in the propagative and receiving dimensions.
           In addition, Edera’s work turns to promoting alternative accesses of its enjoyment in a constructive way, acknowledging in them a close link between participation in cultural events and the growth of a person’s state of well-being and health.

11. Cathartic Dance

           Picking up on Paolo Pecere’s reflection regarding Cathartic Dance, understood as a ritual space and growth of social well-being, Edera believes it is necessary to preserve an indissoluble link between the hedonistic liberation of dance and the cultural importance of events or moments of gathering and sharing.
           The ecstasy of Dance, for this reason, must turn its gaze toward a positive socio-cultural progression and not “marginalize” itself in a bubble of blind celebration and self-induced exclusion.

12. Bacchanalia

           The capitalist conception of social functionality in recent years has shaped in common thinking a vision whereby events of aggregation and cultural enjoyment seem to be entirely separated from the progress of society and its socio-economic facets.
           Edera rejects this vision entirely and believes it is necessary to subvert it through participatory awareness and informative actions, recognizing community union as a virtuous political tool.

13. Necessary Battles

           Edera supports in both symbolism and practical action the pursuit and expansion of socio-cultural well-being and safeness, advancing the ideals of anti-fascism, anti-racism and anti- sexism.
           In order to counter the feelings of hatred and discrimination that still infect the world we live in under various forms, Edera believes it is necessary to carry on a determined and constant battle, both in one’s own microcosm as well as in the support of other realities engaged on the same front.

14. Safe Wellness

           The stigmatization of hedonistic practices no longer has any reason to exist and to condition our expressive pattern: therefore, spaces are needed to enjoy, to express one’s being consciously and to transcend the individuality of the body’s threshold, thus cultivating the duality of joy and revolution as the guiding principle of everyday life.
           Edera has the task and goal of creating a healthy and safe space, respecting the physical and mental well-being of the community, thus becoming a vehicle for healing and community reinforcement.
